## 🛠️ 3. Concrete Production Examples & Specifications

### Core Compliance Protocol
1. **Access Gates**: No modification of core structural code without peer-agent approval.
2. **Review Cycles**: Monthly architectural alignment verification audits.
3. **Escalations**: High severity bugs and incident failures route directly to SRE Leads.


---

## 💡 4. Best Practices
- **Best Practice**: Review governance documents continuously during sprint milestones.
- **Best Practice**: Enforce governance bounds via automated compliance checkers.

---

## ❌ 5. Anti-patterns to Avoid
- **Anti-Pattern**: Allowing fast release loops to bypass structural compliance guidelines.
- **Anti-Pattern**: Storing architectural decisions without corresponding ADR validations.

---

## 🕵️ 6. Automated Quality Gate Review Checklist
- [ ] **Verify**: Confirm all governance guidelines are strictly actionable.

- **Mistake**: Failing to update governance references as platform modules scale.
